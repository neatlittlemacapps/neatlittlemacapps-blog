# Guidelines

Design and development guidelines to ensure consistent, accessible, and user-friendly experiences across all Infinitas Learning products.

## What's Included

- **Accessibility** - WCAG compliance and inclusive design practices
- **Content** - Voice, tone, and writing guidelines
- **Interaction** - Motion, transitions, and micro-interactions
- **Responsive Design** - Mobile-first and adaptive layouts

Follow these guidelines to create experiences that are intuitive, inclusive, and aligned with our brand values.
