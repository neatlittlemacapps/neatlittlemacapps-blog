# Colors

Our color palette is designed to be accessible, consistent, and meaningful across all products.

## Primary Colors

**Brand Blue**
- Hex: `#0066CC`
- RGB: `rgb(0, 102, 204)`
- Usage: Primary actions, links, focus states

**Brand Dark**
- Hex: `#1A1A1A`
- RGB: `rgb(26, 26, 26)`
- Usage: Primary text, headings

## Secondary Colors

**Success Green**
- Hex: `#28A745`
- Usage: Success messages, positive actions

**Warning Orange**
- Hex: `#FFA500`
- Usage: Warnings, caution states

**Error Red**
- Hex: `#DC3545`
- Usage: Error states, destructive actions

## Neutral Colors

- **Gray 100**: `#F8F9FA` - Backgrounds
- **Gray 300**: `#DEE2E6` - Borders
- **Gray 500**: `#6C757D` - Secondary text
- **Gray 900**: `#212529` - Primary text

## Accessibility

All color combinations meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards for contrast ratios (4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text).
