To add to the matter, you've missed their awkward clashing viewpoints on design systems vs dev seats.


Figma is pushing their solution as ideal for creating design systems. One of the advantages of a design system is that devs don't really need to know any of the "specs" of a design anymore. They don't need to bother about colours, spacings, border radius,... That is handled in the components of the design system. They only need to know which component is being used, or rather what variant of that component...


and there's the catch... Devs now need "dev mode" to get the variant info of those components. So you need to pay for all the useless devmode stuff (if you have a design system) just to be able to show them one thing they need to know...
