So here's the thing. After 15+ years bouncing between agency life, consulting gigs, and in-house product teams, I've noticed that the most impactful design work is the stuff nobody sees. The operations, the principles, the documented ways of working that let teams move faster without losing the plot. Get that right, and something clicks... the whole organization starts focusing on what their customers actually need instead of fighting internal friction.

That's what I build. Design systems, design operations, and the strategic frameworks that help organizations think in systems instead of one-off projects.

## The Work

I spend my time in a few interconnected spaces. Building design operations and design systems from scratch (the kind that bridge designers, developers, and product teams without feeling like bureaucracy). Working on UX strategy with organizations trying to figure out where design should actually lead. And increasingly, helping teams think about how AI fits into their design practice in a way that sharpens the human role instead of sidelining it.

That last part matters more than it might seem. AI is changing parts of design, making some things more self-service, pushing the discipline toward orchestration and governance instead of pixel-pushing. But the role doesn't shrink. It pivots. It becomes about defining the principles that shape how systems behave for people, guiding coherence across complexity, and making sure experiences stay human, useful, and responsible at scale.

## How I Got Here

I started as a graphic designer (yep, actual print preflighting at a newspaper... De Morgen, if you're curious). But pretty quickly I realized I was more interested in the bigger picture. How do you actually scale design thinking across an entire organization? How do you build the conditions that let teams move fast without losing coherence? That pulled me from production into digital strategy, then UX, then service design, and eventually into leadership roles.

The trajectory took me through different industries (healthcare, retail, fintech, education) and different operating models. Seven years at The Reference doing service design and UX strategy. A stint at Ogilvy as a senior digital producer. Digital strategy work at Colruyt Group. UX design at Plantyn. Teaching design operations and service design at T-shaped Academy. And most recently, just over three years at Infinitas Learning building design maturity from scratch... establishing the design system and DesignOps practice, with AI embedded in the foundation from day one.
