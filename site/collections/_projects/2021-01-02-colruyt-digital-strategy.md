Design sprint to define a new digital communication strategy for a complex ecosystem of over 40 brands.

The company needed a clear, usable, and supported digital communication strategy. I facilitated the definition of a new vision, mission, and strategic goals — applying the **LEGO Serious Play facilitation technique** among other methods — resulting in simple guiding principles and a framework for continuous adaptation.

The strategy is now clear and supported by all stakeholders. The new framework allows employees to take action and understand what content to create, for whom, and where it should live in the digital ecosystem.
