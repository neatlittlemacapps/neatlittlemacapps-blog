New international web platform catering to architects, engineers, and end-customers.

I held on-site workshops and conducted customer interviews to understand the business, products, and customer segments. I performed customer journey mapping, service blueprinting, and a Jobs to Be Done workshop to define and prioritise solutions — then validated with user testing on prototypes.

The new information architecture caters to multiple segments and provides a solid platform for content strategy — all content is intuitively findable and contextually interconnected.
