300% conversion rate increase by fundamentally rethinking online intake.

The company's online insurance quote request form had a low conversion rate due to too many barriers. I conducted user research and workshops with stakeholders to simplify the process and reduce cognitive load. I considerably grew the number of steps but lowered the cognitive load per step — introducing **motion design** to induce a perceived sense of speed.

The conversion rate improved by **300% on mobile** and **211% on desktop**, far exceeding the estimated 30% improvement.
