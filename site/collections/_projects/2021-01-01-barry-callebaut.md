Unified international multi-brand multi-product online platform.

The company needed to consolidate up to 20 different brands, serving different regions and industries, into one complex website. A key part of this challenge was tailoring the product offering and content to three core industries and their target audiences.

I conducted stakeholder interviews, workshops, and customer journey mapping. To address the challenge of separating product offerings, I created a **smart information architecture** that logically separated the products and content for each industry — also including pathways for audiences to easily explore other industries.

The new information architecture was successfully implemented, which directly addressed the complex product and content segmentation.
