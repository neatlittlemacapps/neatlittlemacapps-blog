Implementing design operations in a new international educational technology group.

Infinitas Learning faced a familiar yet complex problem: how do you raise design maturity across multiple legacy brands without breaking what's already working? Born from merging several publishers, they had multiple live platforms in active development — but no shared operations connecting design, research, and creative work.

I built and scaled a shared design system across educational platforms in a multi-brand, multi-audience domain — introducing DesignOps practices to unify design processes across product, brand, and content teams, and aligning design maturity efforts across multiple European markets.
